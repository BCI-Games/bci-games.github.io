about BCI GAMES

Gaming is for Everyone
Let's Make That a Reality

Games have always been about empowering players to explore new worlds, step into the role of a hero, or just have fun with friends. We believe these experiences should be everyone and that brain-computer interfaces (BCI) will help that become a reality.

Brain-computer interfaces, or BCI for short, use changes in the electric activity of the brain to control a device, like a computer (or a game!) directly just by changing your thoughts or attention. Most BCI systems use a series of small sensors placed on the scalp to read these changes as they happen in real time and is completely safe. No need to worry though - BCIs can only determine the big "broad changes" in your brain, like shifts in attention or imagined movement, not your inner thoughts.
